Questions

Straight Answers. No Runaround.

What do I need to get started? ▼
Just an email address to create your account. To receive payments you'll also need a free Stripe account (takes about 5 minutes to set up at stripe.com). That's it. No design skills, no coding, no tech knowledge required.
What does the 5% commission mean exactly? ▼
For every sale you make, P2P ebook empire takes 5% and you keep 95%. Example: You sell a $20 e-book. You keep $19 (minus Stripe's 2.9% + 30¢ processing fee). P2P ebook empire gets $1. That's it — no surprise fees, no hidden charges. Stripe's fee is separate and goes directly to Stripe, not to us.
How does digital delivery work? ▼
When a customer buys your product, Stripe processes the payment and redirects them to your delivery page automatically. The download link is shown immediately. You don't have to do anything manually — no emails to send, no files to attach. It all happens in seconds, 24/7.
Can I sell memberships and subscriptions? ▼
Yes. Using Stripe's subscription Payment Links, you can create recurring monthly or annual products. Set up a "VIP Community" or "Monthly Content Bundle" and customers are charged automatically every month. Stripe handles all the billing — you just collect the money.
What if I want to cancel? ▼
Cancel anytime from your account settings — no contracts, no penalties, no questions asked. Your store stays active until the end of your billing period. You can export your customer and subscriber data before you leave.
Is this really built for people coming out of incarceration? ▼
Yes — and for everyone who was told the door was closed. BridgeP2P was built specifically for people starting from zero: re-entry, first-generation entrepreneurs, people who never had access to business tools or capital. Your background doesn't matter here. Your products and your hustle do.
