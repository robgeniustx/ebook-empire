Step 4 of 5

Connect Your Payments

P2P ebook empire uses Stripe to process all payments. It's free to set up — you only pay 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction (Stripe's fee, not ours). Our 5% commission is separate.

1

Create a free Stripe account

Go to stripe.com → Click "Start now" → Sign up with your email. Takes 2 minutes. You'll need to verify your identity to receive payouts.

2

Create a Payment Link for each product

In Stripe Dashboard → Payment Links → + New → Set price → Under "Confirmation page" → select "Redirect to website" → Enter: https://yourdomain.com/success.html?product=1

3

Paste your Stripe Payment Link below

Copy the link (looks like https://buy.stripe.com/...) and paste it for your first product.

You can also add this later from Admin → Products → Edit.
⚠️ Important: P2P ebook empire never handles your money directly. All payments go through your Stripe account to your bank. Our 5% commission is tracked separately.
